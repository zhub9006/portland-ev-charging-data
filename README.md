# Portland to Beaverton EV Charging Corridor — Data & Analysis

Field-verified OpenStreetMap charging station data and sustainable commute analysis for the Portland, OR → Beaverton, OR corridor.

## Overview

- **Route:** Downtown Portland, OR → Beaverton, OR ( (~12.8 km / 8 mi)
- **Commute Modes:** Car, Bike, Foot
- **Focus:** EV charging infrastructure and sustainable transportation options

## Key Findings

- **27 charging stations** identified within 5 km of downtown Portland
- **11 stations** within 1 km of city center
- **4+ DC Fast Charging stations** (Tesla Supercharger, EVgo, Electrify America)
- **Bike commute: ~40–50 min** — a practical alternative to driving
- **Sunset Highway corridor gap:** No DC fast chargers between Portland and Beaverton

## Commute Analysis

| Mode | Distance | Duration | Feasibility |
|------|----------|----------|-------------|
| Bike | ~12.8 km | ~40–50 min | ✅ Viable via SW Broadway → Sunset Hwy → SW Beaverton-Hillsdale Hwy |
| Foot | ~12.8 km | ~2.5 hr | ⚠️ Long but possible; not practical for daily commute |
| Car | ~12.8 km | ~15 min | Baseline comparison |
